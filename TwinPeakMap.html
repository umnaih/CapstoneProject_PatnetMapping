Quantitative Twin Peak Map

The two maps provide insights into the number of patents and inventors by country. China stands out as the country with the highest number of patents with a count of 3017, and the highest number of inventors with a count of 10.405k. The United States of America follows China with 1666 patents and 5001 inventors. As we move across other countries, the number of patents and inventors decreases, reaching the lowest value of 1 patent for Norway, Lithuania, and Austria, and the lowest value of 2 inventors for Norway, Denmark, and Austria. These maps suggest that China and the United States of America are leading in both patent and inventor counts, while other countries have significantly lower values.
