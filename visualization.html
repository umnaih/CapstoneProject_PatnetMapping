Patent Mapping

Patent Map is a visual expression of the total analysis results of patents, which helps in understanding complex and diverse patent information easily and effectively.

The patent map is created by collecting relevant patent information from a specific technology field, processing it, and analyzing it.

Patent maps can offer several advantages for various purposes, including administering and planning, technology management, and researching/technology promoting.

For administrative and planning purposes, patent maps can help identify development trends, conduct market research, and determine the relationships between companies.

In terms of technology management, patent maps can be useful in identifying possible infringement, analyzing claims, and setting up a new patent application direction. For research and technology promotion purposes, patent maps can help in identifying technology trends, analyzing technology gaps,and understanding technology relations.
